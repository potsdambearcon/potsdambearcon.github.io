What is Bearcon?

BearCon is an event held at SUNY Potsdam. In a nutshell, BearCon is a convention for 300+ nerds. If you have a nerdy side, this is the place to show it. BearCon is all things anime, manga, video games, sci-fi, and fandoms. Want to have a discussion on StarTrek vs Lord of The Rings? This is the place to do it!


When and where is Bearcon?


November 3-5, 2017 at the SUNY Potsdam Barrington Student Union.


How did Bearcon start?


BearCon originated 6 years ago when the gaming club had the idea to bring nerds together at SUNY Potsdam. Anime club decided to join forces with Gaming Club and collaborate to create an even greater event. Gaming club decided to drop the event in (place date) and the anime club picked it up as the main hosts.
